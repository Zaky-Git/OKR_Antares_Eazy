# Product: Antares Eazy OKR Management System

Type: Internal web-based OKR & initiative management system.

Purpose: Membantu tim Antares Eazy merencanakan, memonitor, dan menyelesaikan objective serta initiative secara terstruktur setiap quarter.

## Problem Statement

- Progress OKR sulit dipantau secara realtime
- Objective dan execution tersebar di spreadsheet/chat
- Tidak ada struktur initiative hierarchy
- Sulit memastikan initiative selesai tepat waktu
- Weekly tracking tidak konsisten
- Tidak ada reminder deadline
- Tidak ada visibility antar individu dan tim

## Product Vision

Membangun sistem manajemen OKR internal yang menghubungkan strategic planning, initiative execution, dan progress monitoring secara terstruktur setiap quarter.

## Business Goals

- Memastikan objective quarter selesai tepat waktu
- Meningkatkan transparansi progress antar tim
- Mengurangi missed initiative
- Mempermudah monitoring PM dan lead
- Menyatukan planning dan execution

## User Types

- Product Manager — creates objectives, monitors progress
- Engineering Lead — monitors team execution, manages key results
- Team Member — executes initiatives, updates progress

## OKR Structure

Product OKR → Team OKR → Individual OKR
Objective → Key Result → Initiative → Child Initiative

## Product Workflow

1. Quarter auto-generated by system
2. OKR Planning — create objectives & key results
3. Sprint Planning — create sprint (set tanggal dalam quarter), assign initiatives ke sprint
4. Sprint Activate — start sprint (hanya 1 active per quarter)
5. Initiative Execution — team update progress
6. Sprint Complete — isi review note + retro note, close sprint
7. Repeat sprint until quarter ends
8. Quarter Review — review overall OKR progress

## MVP Scope

- Auth (login/register)
- Auto quarter management
- Sprint management (manual create, activate, complete with review/retro notes)
- Objective CRUD
- Key Result CRUD
- Initiative tree CRUD (nested, must belong to sprint)
- Progress tracking (auto calculation)
- Due date notification (web in-app)
- Dashboard summary per quarter

## NOT in MVP

- Role-based access control (RBAC)
- Approval workflow
- AI features
- Email notification
- Realtime websocket
- Kanban board
- Gantt chart
- Multi tenant
- Export report
- Advanced analytics

## Expected Impact

- Planning lebih terstruktur
- Visibility execution meningkat
- Initiative lebih mudah dipantau
- Overdue task berkurang
- PM lebih mudah melakukan monitoring
